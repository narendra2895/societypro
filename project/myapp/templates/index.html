About Us

Society Pro was established in the year 2006 as "Society Plus" with a aim of providing a wide range of services of Accounting, Auditing & Management of Co-operative Housing Societies.With growing business and changing time, we decided to rename the organization. We have a team of Management Consultants with very vast knowledge, professional experience and serving its clients and specialized in the field of Co-operative Housing Computerized, Accounting Auditing, Statutory Records. Solving all type of Problems in Co-operative Housing Societies, we undertake assignments with a commitment and make all efforts to deliver Professional & Quality Services in a short time. We are committed to provide tailor made services to the individual needs of each client. Our team provides different range of Services, Expertise & Experience. Our team is backed & directed by Experience Personality "Mrs. Ashwini Pravin Bulakh"

“Co-operative Housing Society Procedure”. Thus we provide appropriate solution for Co-operative Housing Society Management to rely upon. Our core expertise lies in Internal compliance audit, Computerized Accounting, Auditing (internal & external), Legal Compliance, All types of Agreements, Flat Transfer Documents, Re-development/ Construction.
